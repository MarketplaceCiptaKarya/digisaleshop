Shipping, Return, and Cancellation Policy

First and foremost, we encourage you to double-check all details before making a purchase.

Returns are NOT accepted under any circumstances. Returns would result in duplicate shipping and customs fees.

Eligibility Criteria:

Refunds, replacements, or cancellations are considered under certain conditions, including:

Errors in the order contents

Discrepancies between the product and the description or image on the website Prolonged absence or refusal to accept delivery

Other issues caused by the customer's decisions after order confirmation

Defects or damage prior to use

If there are defects or damage before the item is used, please contact us immediately to receive a partial or full refund depending on the item's condition.

Refunds may be issued via gift vouchers or credit card reimbursement depending on the nature of the case.

IMPORTANT NOTE:

Please double-check all personal information you enter, including Full Name, Complete Address, Phone Number, etc., to ensure everything is accurate and complete.

Failed deliveries or lost packages due to incomplete information cannot be replaced or refunded.

Be sure to check your spam or other folders for email updates and order information from us.

We DO NOT ship to P.O. Boxes or military APO/FPO addresses.

For international shipping, customs duties and import taxes are included.

For failed deliveries due to absence, incomplete address, or other issues, the item will be held at the Post Office and you must contact them or the courier. Failure to do so within the specified time may result in your item being returned to us or destroyed.

Process:

  • To begin a refund or replacement process, please contact our customer service team with your order details and the reason for the request.
  • Any changes to the order must be requested within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • Our team will review your request and provide further instructions on how to proceed.
